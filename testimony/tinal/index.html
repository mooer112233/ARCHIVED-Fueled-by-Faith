--- title: Testimony layout: testimony author: Tina Lipps ---
Everything I am and everything that I have is because of Jesus Christ and His immeasurable love.

       My testimony is rather a simple. I was blessed to have grown up in a Christian home all of my life with two loving parents and three older brothers. Since birth, I attended a small church in Western Pennsylvania. When I turned eight years old, I made Jesus Christ Lord of my life by asking Him to be my Savior and direct my life. It was on that day that my life forever changed!

       Since that holy encounter, I have continued to pursue an intimate and growing relationship with Jesus Christ. Without a doubt, I have experienced tough moments and trials throughout my spiritual journey. The Lord has blessed me tremendously, and I am so thankful for His hand upon my life.

       There have been moments in my journey that I have questioned whether or not I have a testimony worth sharing. Satan has tried to twist God's truth into making me believe that because I do not have a big and eventful story of how I came to Christ, I don't have a testimony worth sharing. With that being said, the Lord has affirmed to me that anyone who has experienced the love of Jesus Christ has a story worth telling. I am, after all, for His glory!

       My family and I grew up attending a church camp in Western Pennsylvania called Whitehall Camp and Conference Center. This campground has had a tremendous impact on my spiritual journey and call to ministry. When I turned sixteen years old, I felt God calling me to work full time at this camp on the Summer Ministry Team. It was during that summer that I believe God began to reveal passions and desires that He has given me to be involved with full time ministry.

       God continued to affirm my call to ministry during the next two terms that I served at this campground on staff. It was during my senior year of high school that I decided to wholeheartedly surrender my plans and choose to begin the journey towards becoming a teacher of God's Word.

       After much prayer and petitioning, I took a huge leap of faith and said yes to God's call and nudging for me to study as an undergraduate student at a Christian school in Anderson, Indiana. After graduating from college, God led me to serve in a church as the Associate, Youth Pastor back in Western Pennsylvania. The Lord has continued to prove His faithfulness and His goodness to me everyday of my spiritual journey.

       Every day, I have to remind myself to surrender my plans, dreams and timeline to the Lord. I am learning that His plan truly is best.
